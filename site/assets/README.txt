PHOTOS GO HERE
==============

Drop your images in this folder (site/assets/), then we swap the placeholder
gradients for your real photos. Suggested names so it's easy to wire up:

  hero.jpg            - big home hero (ocean / underwater / you in the field)
  portrait.jpg        - your portrait (for My Journey)
  ocean.jpg           - Ocean world card + page hero
  wetlands.jpg        - Freshwater & Wetlands
  expeditions.jpg     - Expeditions
  healing-nature.jpg  - Healing Nature (forest / calm / dawn light)
  mpa.jpg, seaguard.jpg, batgalim.jpg, herring.jpg  - Ocean project cards

Tips:
- Landscape (wide) shots work best for heroes; ~2000px wide, JPG, under ~500KB each.
- Tell me the filenames and I'll plug them in (or I can guide you).
