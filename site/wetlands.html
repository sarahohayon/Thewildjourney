World 02 · Freshwater & Wetlands

The waters that hold everything together.

Vanishing pools, living streams, and the communities bringing them back to life.

Wetlands are the planet's quiet workhorses — nurseries, filters, sponges against flood and drought. I've spent years mapping, restoring, and defending them, always alongside the people who live beside them.

This world is being built

Coming here: the national vernal-pools conservation program, stream restoration, the "Business & Community for Local Biodiversity" grassland project, and the citizen-science teams who made it happen — with photos and films from the field.
